here are the great things about devops
1. DevOps is a combination of software development and operations.
2. It is an approach to software development and delivery that emphasizes collaboration between development and operations teams.
3. DevOps helps to reduce the time it takes to develop, test, deploy and maintain applications.
4. The primary goal of DevOps is to improve the speed and quality of software releases while reducing costs.
5. DevOps requires a culture shift to foster collaboration and communication between development and operations teams.
6. DevOps requires a shift in focus from traditional software development processes to automation.
7. DevOps requires the use of tools such as configuration management, continuous  integration and continuous delivery.
8. DevOps requires a culture of shared responsibility and accountability among all members of the team.
9. DevOps also requires a focus on quality assurance, security, and scalability.
10. DevOps is an iterative process that seeks to improve existing processes and systems over time.
